Empowering Sustainable Fleets

Boosted Batteries, powered by ZF Friedrichshafen, transforms used commercial vehicle batteries into cost-effective, long-lasting energy storage solutions for electrified fleets.

Explore Our Solutions

What We Do

We take dismissed batteries from ZF commercial vehicles and give them a second life. Our battery racks, powered by off-peak electricity, charge a retrofitted electric fleet at a fraction of the cost of new EV investments. With our partner TOLV, we ensure your vehicles are efficiently converted and maintained, reducing environmental impact and extending battery life indefinitely.

Cost-Effective

Our solution costs significantly less than buying a new electric fleet or performing retrofitting with traditional batteries.

Unlimited Battery Life

Our refurbished battery trays are designed for easy cell replacement every few months, ensuring a virtually unlimited lifespan.

Environmental Impact

By reusing battery cells and maintaining them regularly, we reduce waste and lower the environmental footprint of commercial transport.
