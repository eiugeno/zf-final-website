Pricing & Cost Advantages

Simple, Predictable, and Cost-Effective

Our business model is straightforward. For each vehicle you retrofit (at a cost of approximately €20,000 through TOLV), you pay a pay-per-kWh tariff of around €0.65 per kWh consumed. This fee covers energy, maintenance, and the battery cost itself, locking in predictable, long-term expenses.

Comparing the Costs

  • **New EV Fleet Purchase:** ~€85,000 per vehicle initial investment
  • **Traditional Retrofitting:** ~€30,000 per vehicle initial investment
  • **Boosted Batteries & TOLV Retrofitting:** ~€20,000 per vehicle initial investment + €0.65/kWh

The difference is clear: our solution significantly lowers your upfront costs and ensures you’re not burdened by frequent battery replacements. Our modular design and maintenance program allow the battery to last indefinitely, unlike conventional batteries that need replacing every 5 years.
