Our Fleet Electrification Solutions

From Dismissed Batteries to Efficient Charging

Our core solution is to repurpose dismissed batteries from ZF commercial vehicles. Once their capacity falls below 80%, we reconfigure them into large-scale battery racks—ranging up to 6000 kWh. These racks can store off-peak electricity at lower rates, providing a stable and affordable energy source for your converted electric vehicles.

Partnership with TOLV

We’ve partnered with TOLV, a startup specializing in retrofitting existing internal combustion engine (ICE) commercial vehicles into electric ones. Together, we can transform your current fleet into a zero-emissions one. This holistic approach ensures a seamless transition and improved total cost of ownership.
