Our Technology

Modular & Maintainable Battery Trays

Our battery trays, each with approximately 52 kWh capacity, are designed with modularity in mind. Every three months, we inspect every cell to identify defective ones. By replacing these cells promptly, we prevent capacity loss in neighboring cells, extending the overall battery life indefinitely.

Continuous Improvement & Data Analysis

We leverage data analytics to monitor battery health, usage patterns, and performance. This allows us to optimize maintenance schedules and improve the quality of refurbished cells over time, ensuring maximum uptime for your fleet.
